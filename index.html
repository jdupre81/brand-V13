Identity & Brand Guidelines

Renku began in 2017, originally as Renga and then renamed to Renku (Renga had already been registered for use in an adjacent domain). Both terms refer to styles of Japanese poetry, where the poem is created collaboratively, with the input of many people. That seemed like an evocative metaphor for data science projects, which also require the collaboration of many people with different expertise (domain knowledge, machine-learning expertise, software engineering, etc.) to realize the best results. Though some of details for how we want to achieve this have been in flux, the high-level goal has always been for Renku to be a platform for enabling collaborative data science.


What is Renku?


Renku is a platform that bundles together various tools for reproducible and collaborative data analysis projects. It is aimed at independent researchers and data scientists as well as labs, collaborations, and courses and workshops. Organisation & structure


Renku has a non-hierarchical organisation. It is developed as an open source project led by a dedicated team at the Swiss Data Science Center based at EPFL and ETHZ. Renku team is made up of 18 people from different nationalities, organised in 6 teams: Leadership, Product , UI, Renku Python, Knowledge Graph and DevOps.

Our Renku Identity


Our Renku Identity provides a consistent, logical, and satisfying user experience that fulfills the promise of Renku's unique value proposition. Renku Identity is composed by four 3 categories: Culture, Brand & Design, Behaviour and Communication. In this document, each category contains description about the meaning and use of the brand and its relationship with Renku core values.

Our Culture

Renku members share common values and principles based on respect, collaboration, honesty, trust, ingenuity, accountability and simplicity. The workplace environment is not only familiar, open and friendly but also fresh, positive, cooperative and supportive.

Brand & Design

Brand principles


The brand principles describes the core values and characteristics that has been taken in consideration to build Renku brand’s identity. Renku principles based on respect, ingenuity, open and friendly collaboration are the basis for all internal and external (written, oral and visual) communication.


Tone of voice


Renku’s tone of voice is positive, supportive and friendly. Team members communicate with a open and friendly attitude. Collaboration is one of main values of Renku team and this must be reflected in all communication channels, written, oral and visual.
